The Ultimate Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Tomato products: 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, AND 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar (to cut acidity)
  • Spice and seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • An egg (to bind the ricotta)

How to actually make it?

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna (According to instructions in next session)
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest.
  7. Enjoy!?!?

How to layer your lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmsesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, and top with remaining Parmesan
